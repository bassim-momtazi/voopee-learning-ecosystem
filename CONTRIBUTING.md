  # How to Contribute to Voopee

    Thank you for your interest in contributing to Voopee! We are building an innovative adaptive learning ecosystem and appreciate your support.

    ## Current Status

    Voopee is currently in the pre-development and strategic planning phase. As such, we are not accepting direct code contributions at this moment.

    ## How You Can Help

    While we are not seeking code contributions, there are many other ways you can support the project:

    *   **Feedback & Ideas:** If you have ideas, suggestions, or feedback on the project's philosophy and roadmap outlined in the `README.md`, please [open an issue](https://github.com/[YourUsername]/[YourRepoName]/issues) and let us know.
    *   **Potential Partnership:** If you are a developer, an educator, or an investor interested in collaborating, please reach out to us directly at [your.email@example.com].

    We are actively looking to build our core team. If our vision resonates with you, we would love to hear from you.
